About Dr. Sharma

Dr. Rajesh Sharma is one of Nepal's most experienced veterinarians with over 15 years of dedicated service to animal health. He completed his Doctor of Veterinary Medicine degree from Tribhuvan University and has since treated thousands of dogs across Nepal. Dr. Sharma is particularly passionate about emergency care and has saved countless lives through his quick thinking and expert surgical skills. He believes in preventive care and works closely with pet owners to ensure their dogs live long, healthy lives.
