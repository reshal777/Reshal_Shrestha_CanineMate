Smart Health Tracking

Maintain a complete digital health vault for your pet. Track every vaccination, log past clinic visits, and manage life-saving medicine reminders with automated notifications designed to keep your pet safe.

Skip the queues and uncertainty. Book appointments with recognized, verified veterinarians across Nepal. View available slots in real-time and complete secure payments instantly.
