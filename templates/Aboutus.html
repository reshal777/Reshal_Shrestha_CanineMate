🐾 About CanineMate

Nepal's Smart Dog Care Platform

CanineMate is Nepal's first all-in-one digital platform built exclusively for dog owners. We make it effortless to manage your dog's health records, book verified veterinarians, schedule grooming sessions, shop for premium pet products, and find loving homes for rescue dogs — all from one place. Whether you're a first-time pet parent or an experienced dog owner, CanineMate is your trusted companion every step of the way.

Health Tracking

Keep your dog's entire medical history organised in one place. Log vaccinations, health visits, and set medicine reminders — so you never miss a critical care moment for your pet.

Vet Appointments

Book consultations with verified veterinarians across Nepal in seconds. Pick your vet, choose a time slot, and complete payment online — no phone calls, no waiting.

Grooming Services

Schedule professional grooming sessions at trusted salons near you. From baths and haircuts to nail trimming — keep your dog clean, comfortable, and always looking their best.

Dog Adoption

Help rescue dogs find loving homes. Browse verified adoption listings, post your own dog for adoption, and connect with families ready to give a dog their forever home across Nepal.

Pet Shop

Shop quality dog food, toys, accessories, and health supplies — all handpicked for your pet. Order online and get it delivered to your door anywhere in Nepal.
